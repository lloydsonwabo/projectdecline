About Us

Incline and Decline is a youthowned service provider of civil engineering infrastructure solutions. Our mainarea of operation is currently the Eastern Cape Province, with the aims of expanding to the rest of South Africa. The company was founded in 2007, it was formed primarily to provide services to the people and also to provide job opportunities for the people uplifting their standard of living. The strength of the company is its ability to provide excellent and efficient services to every customer. It composes of various individuals who have vast experience in the field of construction.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To construct both rural and urban civil society infrastructure such as gravel roads, water and sewer reticulationand general building with implementation of government designed construction works. Improve the quality of life of both rural, semi-urban areas and urban communities of South Africa through provision of high quality construction services. Our principle of community participation is based on Batho Pele Principles and improvement of the society to create an atmosphere of independence, mutual trust and sharing of knowledge.We are committed to generating funds and plough some of our profits back to the community based projects.

Our Vision

Our visionis to build a very strong, efficient, innovative company that will not only offer service of high quality to the satisfaction of everybody but create awareness by reaching as far as the rural and urban areas, extracting all knowledge and talent that the province has to offer. Continue sincrease in black youth entrepreneurship and individuals development to ensure prosperity and stability in businesses that is proud and confident to compete in world markets exposure and constant competitive confidence, team building and with leadership qualities. Assist with poverty alleviation in our communities through job creation opportunities during infrastructure development projects.

Our Services

Civil Works & General Buildings

With the material we own, we construct buildings or infrastructure with high quality. We build bridges, schools, roads, etc.

Plant Hire & Small Equipments

Incline and Decline is a sevice industry providing machinery, equipment and tools of all kinds and sizes for a limited period of time to final users, mainly to construction contractors but also to industry and individual consumers..

Road Maintenance

Remedying defects such as potholes that occur in the carriageway from time to time and providing treatments such as cracks sealing which will slow the rate of deterioration is one of our offers.

Fencing & Glass Cutting

We also build the barriers enclosing or bordering a field, yard, etc. We also have tools to do glass cutting to desired patterns and sizes
